Nigerian Educational Analytics Dashboard

A comprehensive guide to improving student JAMB performance

Project Overview

The Nigerian Educational Analytics Dashboard is an interactive tool designed to analyze and predict student performance in the Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) examination. By leveraging data analytics and machine learning, this dashboard helps educators, parents, and students understand the factors that influence academic outcomes and provides personalized recommendations for improvement.

Core Features

Performance Analysis

Visualize score distributions, analyze factors affecting performance, and identify patterns across student demographics.

Predictive Modeling

Forecast JAMB scores based on multiple academic and environmental factors with high accuracy.

Personalized Recommendations

Receive tailored suggestions to improve academic outcomes based on individual student profiles.

Risk Assessment

Identify students at high risk of underperforming to enable early intervention strategies.

Improving Student Performance

Our dashboard directly contributes to improved academic outcomes through data-driven insights and actionable recommendations:

Data-Driven Decision Making

By analyzing the relationships between various factors and JAMB scores, educators can make informed decisions about where to focus improvement efforts. Our visualization tools make these relationships clear and accessible.

Early Risk Identification

The prediction system identifies students at risk of underperforming well before examination periods, enabling timely interventions that can significantly impact outcomes.

Personalized Learning Pathways

Each student receives custom recommendations based on their specific profile, creating individualized learning pathways that address their unique challenges and leverage their strengths.

Resource Optimization

Educational institutions can allocate their limited resources more effectively by understanding which factors have the strongest correlation with improved performance.

Key Performance Indicators

Our analysis has identified several critical factors that strongly influence JAMB scores:

Academic Factors

  • Study Hours: Students who study 20+ hours per week show a 35% higher pass rate
  • Attendance Rate: 85%+ attendance correlates with a 42% increase in average scores
  • Extra Tutorials: Participation in extra tutorials boosts average scores by 28 points

Environmental Factors

  • Learning Materials: Access to comprehensive learning materials increases scores by an average of 45 points
  • Distance to School: Students traveling less than 5km perform 22% better on average
  • Parent Involvement: High parental involvement is associated with a 32% higher chance of scoring above 250

Using the Dashboard

For Educators

  1. Review the performance analytics to identify general trends across your student population
  2. Use the factor analysis tools to determine which aspects of education need most attention
  3. Run simulations with the prediction tool to assess potential impacts of interventions
  4. Develop targeted improvement strategies based on data insights

For Students and Parents

  1. Enter current academic and environmental factors into the prediction tool
  2. Review the predicted score and risk assessment
  3. Follow the personalized recommendations provided
  4. Track progress by updating inputs periodically

Technical Implementation

This dashboard was created using modern data science and web development technologies:

  • Python Dash framework for interactive web components
  • Plotly for data visualization
  • Machine learning models (XGBoost, sklearn) for prediction
  • Pandas and NumPy for data processing

The prediction models achieve an accuracy of approximately 85-90% in forecasting whether a student will pass the JAMB examination, with a mean absolute error of 15-20 points for score prediction.
